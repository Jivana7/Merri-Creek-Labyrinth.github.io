Community

Friends of the Merri Creek Labyrinth (FMCL) was established to care for the space in 2011. The ‘Friends of the Merri Creek labyrinth’ was formed to help maintain the space and encourage its use as a free public space. We gather for occasional working bees… To keep informed join our email list or social platforms Pls contact us if you have any questions or would like to get involved! Contact us!

Connection

We are a grassroots community oriented project. We are always looking for like minded people to help the friends grow! If this sounds like something that interestes you please contact us we are always looking for people to get involved with us to help tend this beautiful space.

Cooperation

The Friends are local residents and from surrounding regions. We gather for occasional working bees and network informally at the labyrinth or over coffee. We invite you to join our Friends email list and participate as you are able. Join next work Bee!
