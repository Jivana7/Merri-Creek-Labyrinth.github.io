Up coming Events and Work Bees...

Planting Day 2022

We are having a Community Planting Day this Sunday! With funding from City of Yarra, and hands-on support from their biodiversity contractor Felix Botanica, and admin backup from Friends of Merri Creek, we are looking forward to seeing the next garden bed around the Labyrinth take shape. Come and help us with the planting of indigenous plants and grasses on Sunday the 15th May. Please register for the day at https://www.eventbrite.com.au/.../merri-creek-labyrinth... or scan QR code on the flyer for details. Register and come along!

Autumn Festival 2017

The Labyrinth Autumn Festival is on next Saturday 3pm - Dusk!!! Come and support the Labyrinth as a free public asset and celebrate the Autumn Equinox. Will be a great afternoon! The Labyrinth is a valued local resource for enhancing health and well-being. It’s a space where individuals and a community can reflectively meditate on their lives - within the context of an urban yet natural environment, a place to go that makes you feel better...

Picnic at the Labyrinth

The Friends of the Labyrinth wish to invite the community for an afternoon in the park. Lets celebrate the Labyrinth and the beautiful Merri Creek and community. Relax with others in a most lovely enviroment and enjoy the afternoon. Bring a Picnic basket, blankets nibbles and drinks. Their will be music and time for reflection and a guded Labyrinth walk!
